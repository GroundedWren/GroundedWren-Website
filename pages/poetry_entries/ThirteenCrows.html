Thirteen Crows

I can’t stand this self
Rip that picture off the shelf
I kick and kick but still I sink
My heart of lead my mind of ink
Wood to ash and ash to wood
In the yard are all who stood
Now hanging dead from burning trees
Strike me blind it hurts to see
Fear the ancient one who tugs
Upon the strings of man and bugs
Of pain and love he is the source
She’s the one who runs our course
Shut her off, shut him out
Make me numb to all the doubt
Nestled deep in fired coal
Lost myself within the role
A dozen crows take somber flight
As I slink into the lonesome night
Fly fly away
Fly fly away
