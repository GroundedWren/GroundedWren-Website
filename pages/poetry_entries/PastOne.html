Past One

By Vera @ GroundedWren.com 8/9/23

Nestled into befallen dark
Seeped in our skulls, behind dulled eyes
We sit together landed, languished, lightless
We are conductors within each other
A spark spreads unabated
Connected, catalyzed, cremated
Scarred similarity
Public private pain
I'll show you yours if you show me mine
Why do we not weep
Since we see in foregone sleep
There is nothing, nothing
Nothing unthought
