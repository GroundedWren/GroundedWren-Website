Molasses is Sweet

The mood hung around like the steam from a shower
Hot into cold and from thick into thin
Clinging to all creeping slowly within
Ten thousand years just to slide from the bed
Eons to work and still longer in spin
But seconds or instants now to fill up with gin
A cup dunked in ether that classic old friend
It empties and sighs as it tilts further back
Shrieking to still and blinding to black
I just heard this long track crawling in from the breach
The rebound on shift now from flight to attack
It’s bound up again all reset to unpack
And I still need it
