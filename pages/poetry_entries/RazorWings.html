Razor Wings

By Vera @ GroundedWren.com

Close the door and flick the light
Left alone I’ll make this right
Gliding down in careful sweep
Take my hair but flesh I’ll keep
Tiny step for me a leap
Scarlet ink slides down my skin
Seems the blade has caught my shin
Metal taste and flower scent
Blood and cream a sad lament
Razor wings on the ascent
Cut away this atmosphere
Razor wings always sincere
Tears that can’t fall from my eyes
White blood cells in blood that dries
Razor wings to slice what ties
