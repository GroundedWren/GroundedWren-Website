Night Flight

By Vera @ GroundedWren.com 8/5/23

No noise
'cept the muted jet roar
And the slow-dying whisper of faraway voices
No light
'cept the dull blue-grey glow
And what little pierces our eyecover fabric
No touch
'cept the cruel gnawing aches
And your fumbling hand grasping blindly for mine
No words
'cept the ones that we know
And what we can say with our fingerpad mouths
"Just know I'm with you"
"We'll be alright"
