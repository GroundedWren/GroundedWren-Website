Under the Macroscope

Can you recall what is the word
For rocks with purple quartz within
For birds who stay but know the way
And bugs whose songs are seldom heard
I know quite well that searing word
Hurled by some to strike and spark
And caught by more to stand upon
The sound entwined with color blurred
And love or hate thoughts of this word
Within your head you have a box
With borderlines of what can be
You analyze and group and herd
I can’t escape or shun this word
It’s latched itself to my headwires
It classifies but can’t control
And once it’s said can’t be unheard
Are you ready
Am I ready
And will you try to understand
I’m not ready
Head unsteady
Is this a thing that can be planned
