Melting

By Vera @ GroundedWren.com

As the cold recedes it leaves its marks
Glacial features in the parks
Cobbled pavement on the streets
Deep imprints into the sheets
On how many journeys must one go
How much time no more alone
Until they go away
Driven out on skipping stones
How many years which have begun to flow
Tending plants as they are grown
Just to drive it all away
Sucked out with marrow from my bones
As the sun bleaches the heat teaches
Nothing can unfreeze this snow
I guess slush is a pain anyway
