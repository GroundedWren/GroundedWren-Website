The Jester

By Vera @ GroundedWren.com

Of tattooed wings the jester sings
And thoughts that never last
Serpents, kings, and all these things
Dug up from the past
Spinning wheels and drive-thru meals
A force of timeless mass
Movie reels and busted keels
Buried ‘neath the grass
All he tells and all he yells
A mess of arcane thoughts
It rings a bell and casts a spell
Time itself here knots
