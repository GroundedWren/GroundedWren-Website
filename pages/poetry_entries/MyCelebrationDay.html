My Celebration Day

By Vera @ GroundedWren.com

Light the fires, burn the clouds
Spare the liars, calm the crowds
We’re gonna have a celebration
Let us not forget the days
Lost in mud and ash below
Let us sing and praise the ways
Warm rain falls as soft as snow
One part oil, nine parts cure
Dash of soil, passion pure
This is the drink of celebration
Having played the keys of time
I will gaze long down the road
Filled with awe of freedom’s chime
Gleaming beams of sound echoed
Close the door, turn the key
Step in four, talk in three
This is the dance of celebration
Pillars tall will stay behind
Bearing weight that is no more
By themselves they were designed
And from rock they will restore
Oust the fires, draw the clouds
Paint the liars, leave the crowds
We’re gonna have a celebration
