Ink Black Tea

From the darkness of night I look up
How has this come to be
With a sigh I will pour in my cup
This hot and bitter tea
When I left all my friends wished me well
They said I must be free
Since I’ve gone they've moved on, I can tell
So I just sip my tea
I’m just used by each man ‘fore he goes
Like waves who roam the sea
My bones crack with the pace of the blows
I cough up scalding tea
I’m so lost and so far from my home
I’m starved and cannot see
I’ve been cursed so I’m ever to roam
Choked by this ink black tea
