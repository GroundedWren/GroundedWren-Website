Continuum

Limit the options and build the fences
To see how the mind extricates
Twisting and bending and shaping new form
Understate, subtilize, sneak beyond what is there
See the individual chef from a timeless dish
Accented and accessorized in little whispered ways
What is trope what’s cliché and what can truly be yours
The thought pool is vast but the words countable
Iterate and permutate to reach all indexed thoughts
So select your sample and just hope it’s what fits
Plucked from a place it can’t be removed
Maybe you wrote it down; it exists on its own
Still somehow it carries you along
Different to all but in print the same
