A Road to Chult

By Vera @ GroundedWren.com 6/12/22

Summary

Woe to those who seek to conquer Death herself.

A young wizard apprentice is destined to journey alone to Chult, not for any wealth or glory, but in a desperate bid to undo her own mistakes. No training can prepare her for the painful and bloody path she must walk before her real adventure can even begin.

Notes

This is a player character backstory to a Tomb of Annihilation campaign I'm in.

It exists in a different universe from my Adventures of The Risen series, though some characters carry over.

Content warning: blood and death.
